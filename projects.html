Superstore Sales Executive Dashboard

This project started as separate dashboards for sales, regional performance, product analysis, and forecasting. The main challenge was combining them into a single view without overwhelming the user. I approached this by designing an executive dashboard focused on clarity and decision-making. Instead of showing everything, I prioritized the most important signals — overall performance, key drivers, and future outlook. In addition to historical analysis, I incorporated forecasting models (ARIMA and Prophet) to provide a forward-looking perspective. This allows the dashboard to answer not just what happened, but what is likely to happen next. Through this project, I focused on structuring information effectively, simplifying complex insights, and aligning the dashboard with real business needs.

  • Designed a KPI-focused layout for quick executive insights
  • Analyzed regional, segment, and product-level performance
  • Identified loss-making areas impacting profitability
  • Integrated sales and profit forecasting using time series models
