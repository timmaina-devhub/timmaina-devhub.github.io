Superstore Sales Executive Dashboard

A single-screen command centre that turns four years of Superstore sales data into executive-ready insights — combining performance analysis with forward-looking forecasts so decision-makers never have to dig through a spreadsheet again.

The Problem

Leadership needed visibility into which regions, segments, and product categories were driving — or dragging — profitability, with no easy way to project where sales were heading.

My Approach

Built a KPI-focused Tableau dashboard consolidating sales, profit, and order data. Layered in ARIMA/Prophet time series models for 6-month sales forecasting, and flagged loss-making categories directly on the viz.

Key Outcomes

  • Identified loss-making sub-categories dragging overall profit margin
  • Regional drill-downs revealing underperforming territories
  • Forecast integration enabling proactive stock and budget planning
  • Reduced time-to-insight from hours of Excel work to one dashboard

Customer Churn Analysis & Segmentation

Why do customers leave — and who is most at risk right now? This end-to-end project answers both questions using machine learning and RFM segmentation, turning raw e-commerce transaction data into a clear retention playbook.

The Problem

The business had no systematic way to identify which customers were drifting toward churn or to distinguish loyal customers from one-time buyers — making retention efforts scattershot and expensive.

My Approach

Combined RFM feature engineering with a logistic regression churn model optimised for recall — ensuring high-risk customers are caught, not missed. K-Means clustering then grouped customers into actionable segments (loyal, at-risk, inactive) surfaced through an interactive dashboard.

Key Outcomes

  • Churn model with strong recall — high-risk customers effectively flagged
  • Inactivity and refund behaviour identified as strongest churn signals
  • 3 distinct customer segments enabling targeted retention campaigns
  • Interactive dashboard connecting insights directly to business actions

Fraud Detection Command Centre

Financial fraud hides in behavioural patterns — not just transaction amounts. This end-to-end system engineers the right signals, trains a model to catch them, and surfaces everything through a real-time Tableau command centre built for fraud analysts.

The Problem

Rule-based fraud detection flags too many false positives and misses evolving patterns. The challenge was building a behavioural model that could detect fraud accurately on imbalanced, imperfect real-world data — then communicate findings to non-technical stakeholders.

My Approach

Engineered behavioural features — transaction velocity, device/IP repetition, and time-window anomalies. Applied synthetic sampling to handle class imbalance, compared three models, and selected Random Forest for its precision-recall balance. Output fed directly into a Tableau command centre.

Key Outcomes

  • Random Forest model with strong precision-recall on imbalanced data
  • High-frequency burst patterns identified as primary fraud signal
  • Device & IP repetition flagging catching repeat fraudsters
  • Tableau dashboard showing fraud trends, high-risk entities & financial impact
