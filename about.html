Who I Am

I'm Timothy Maina, a data analyst based in Nairobi with a passion for turning messy, complex datasets into clear, actionable insights. I believe data tells a story — my job is to find it and communicate it in a way that drives real decisions.

My work sits at the intersection of analytics, visualization, and machine learning. Whether it's building interactive dashboards, segmenting customers, or training forecasting models, I focus on outputs that are not just technically sound but genuinely useful to the people who rely on them.

I'm currently deepening my expertise in machine learning and data science, with the goal of building predictive systems that scale. I thrive in environments where curiosity is valued and data is taken seriously.

What Drives Me

I got into data analytics because I wanted to do work that matters. Numbers without context are noise — but with the right framing, they become the clearest language in any room. That gap between raw data and confident decision-making is exactly where I want to operate.
